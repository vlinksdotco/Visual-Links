About:

VLinks (formerly Visual Links) - It is a Visual, Social and Anonymous web search engine created ( and still evolving) to search for information on Internet
with a visual display of search results (Visual aspect). Search results presented with list of logos (visual links) in "visual way" and not in an old "text way" (a lot of text links) like other search engines.The content is contributing by users - you can add your own favorite sites (Social aspect). We do not collect or share personal information. We do not tracking you and care about your privacy (Anonymous aspect).
